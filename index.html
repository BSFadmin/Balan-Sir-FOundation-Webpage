Remembering Balan Sir

"A teacher's influence never fades."

Er. P Balan, our beloved Balan Sir was not just an educator; he was a guiding light who shaped countless lives through his wisdom, patience, and unwavering dedication. His tenure, spanning over three decades, in CARMEL POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE, PUNNAPRA, ALAPPUZHA, left an indelible mark on his students, colleagues, and the community. He championed the belief that true education extends beyond textbooksâ€”it encompasses character development, critical thinking, and empathy.

His classroom was a crucible where curiosity met purpose, transforming countless students into not just exceptional engineers, but compassionate leaders and ethical trailblazers.

We invite you to join us in celebrating his extraordinary life and contributing to the continuation of his legacy.

About the Foundation

The Balan Sir Foundation was established by his former students to ensure his philosophy lives on. We strive to support deserving students, fund educational initiatives, and host community programs that reflect the integrity and human kindness Balan Sir embodied every day.

Through this foundation, we honour his indomitable spirit by nurturing innovation, fostering integrity, and empowering generations to carry forward his ethos of excellence, service, and boundless empathy.

Here, his light lives on guiding minds, igniting dreams, and perpetuating a legacy where education transcends skill to sculpt souls.
